Essential Travel Tips

Know before you go to the Kingdom in the Sky.

Accommodation

From mountain lodges to cultural villages, Lesotho offers unique stays. Popular options include:

  • Maliba Mountain Lodge (luxury)
  • Semonkong Lodge (adventure base)
  • Basotho Cultural Village (authentic)
  • Backpackers in Maseru (budget)

Book in advance during peak season (May–September).

Transport

Getting around Lesotho requires planning. Best options:

  • 4x4 vehicle (essential for mountain roads)
  • Local taxis & minibuses (cheap but crowded)
  • Guided tours (stress-free exploration)
  • Pony trekking (unique & traditional)

Local Culture & Etiquette

Respect Basotho traditions to enrich your visit:

  • Greeting: Say "Lumela" (hello) and "Khotso" (peace)
  • Dress: Modestly in villages; wear Basotho blanket in highlands
  • Photography: Always ask permission before taking photos of people
  • Gifts: Small tokens for village hosts appreciated (sugar, tea)

Learning a few Sesotho words opens many doors.
