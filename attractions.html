Maluti Mountains

Throughout Lesotho

The spine of Lesotho, these ancient mountains offer breathtaking scenery, winding passes, and traditional villages. The highest peak, Thabana Ntlenyana (3,482m), is the highest point in southern Africa. Perfect for trekking and pony trekking.
